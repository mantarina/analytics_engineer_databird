{% docs mrt_local_bike__sales_and_discount_per_product_and_store %}

This model generates some key metrics on sales and discounts at the level of products, product categories and stores:
- total_orders_per_product_store = number of orders placed 
- total_discount_per_product_store = total amount of discount 
- average_discount_per_product_store = average discount rate 
- total_amount_per_product_store = total amount of orders, discount included

the purpose is to offer some solid metrics that will help business to take concrete actions
